About


Trading Post is where people can obtain new or used products without spending a dime. We are a bartering system that allows users to trade their own goods or services with other users. Our mission is to create an alternative to the monetary system and strengthen bonds within local communities. Want to get started? Go to our sign up page to create your own profile. Then search for goods or services using the search bar and begin trading with other users!
